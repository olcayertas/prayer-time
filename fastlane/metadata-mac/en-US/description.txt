Namaz Vakti lives in your Mac's menu bar with a live countdown to the next prayer, powered by the published tables of the Turkish Directorate of Religious Affairs (Diyanet).

Clean, fast, and private — no account, no ads, no tracking.

IN YOUR MENU BAR
• A live, glanceable countdown to the next prayer.
• A dropdown with today's six times — İmsak, Güneş, Öğle, İkindi, Akşam, Yatsı — the next one highlighted, plus the Hijri and Gregorian date.

A FULL WINDOW
• Today, with a large countdown and the day's six prayer times.
• A spacious Monthly view: a Focus Card for the selected day over a compact month chart — click any day to promote it.

YOUR LOCATION, YOUR WAY
• Automatic: the app finds your city and shows the right times wherever you are.
• Pinned: choose a city by hand (country → city → district) and keep it.
• Each location is cached, so it works offline and makes no unnecessary requests.

WIDGET & NOTIFICATIONS
• A Notification Center widget with the next prayer and a live countdown.
• Optional notifications at each prayer time.

BEAUTIFUL & ACCESSIBLE
• Two themes: the clean system look (auto light/dark) and a warm dark "Arc" theme.
• Full Dynamic Type and VoiceOver support.
• English, Turkish, and Arabic.
